Privacy Policy for Focus! Workout Planner

1. Introduction

Focus! Workout Planner is a simple workout planning app designed to help users create exercises, build workouts, use timers, track basic progress, and manage their training routines without social media features, accounts, or unnecessary clutter.

Your privacy is important. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app does and does not collect, how workout data is handled, and how users can contact the developer.

2. No Account Required

Focus! Workout Planner does not require users to create an account.

The app does not use login systems, user profiles, social accounts, friend lists, followers, or community features.

3. Personal Information Collection

Focus! Workout Planner does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

The app does not collect information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Home address
  • Date of birth
  • Payment information
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos or videos
  • Health records
  • Social media information

4. Workout Data

The app may allow users to create and save workout-related information, such as:

  • Custom exercises
  • Custom workout routines
  • Timer and rest settings
  • Basic workout progress
  • Simple graph data
  • Backup or export files created by the user

This information is stored locally on the user’s device unless the user chooses to export or back up the data manually.

The developer does not receive, access, or store this workout data on external servers.

5. Backup and Export

Focus! Workout Planner may include backup and export features so users can save or transfer their own workout data.

Any backup or export file is created and controlled by the user. The user is responsible for where they save, share, or store exported files.

The developer does not automatically upload backup files or exported data to any server.

6. Internet Connection

Focus! Workout Planner is designed to work as a simple workout planning tool.

If the app does not use internet features, then no workout data is sent online by the app.

If future versions include internet-based features, this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain what data is used and why.

7. Third-Party Services

Focus! Workout Planner does not use social media tracking, advertising profiles, or account-based third-party services.

If the app uses standard platform services such as Google Play billing for the paid version, Google may process purchase-related information according to Google’s own policies. The developer does not receive or store full payment details.

8. Children’s Privacy

Focus! Workout Planner is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Because the app does not require accounts and does not collect personal information, the developer does not knowingly collect data from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact the developer so the issue can be reviewed.

9. Data Sharing

Focus! Workout Planner does not sell, rent, trade, or share user personal information.

Because the app does not collect personal information, there is no personal information to share with third parties.

10. Data Security

Workout data created in the app is stored locally on the user’s device.

Users are responsible for keeping their device secure and for safely managing any backup or export files they create.

11. Data Deletion

Because Focus! Workout Planner does not use accounts or external servers, the developer cannot access or delete user data remotely.

Users can delete their workout data by:

  • Deleting exercises or workouts inside the app, if available
  • Clearing the app’s data from Android settings
  • Uninstalling the app
  • Deleting any backup or export files they created

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time, especially if new features are added to the app.

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
